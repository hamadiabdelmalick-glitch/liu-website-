Admissions

Learn about admission requirements, deadlines and how to apply.

Undergraduate Admissions

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Recommended GPA: 3.0+
  • Transcripts, personal statement, recommendation letters

Graduate Admissions

  • Bachelor's degree in a related field
  • Recommended GPA: 3.2+
  • Transcripts, CV, statement of purpose, references

Deadlines & Fees

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. International applicants should apply at least 3 months before the term.

Scholarships & Aid

Merit and need-based aid available; consult the financial aid office for eligibility and deadlines.
